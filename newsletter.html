About the Newsletter

Written for people working with international trade.

Trade Over Coffee is published monthly by Darren Welsh, founder of DS4 Trade & Customs Advisory.

The newsletter focuses on practical business implications rather than simply repeating regulatory announcements. Each edition aims to explain what developments mean, where the risks sit and what businesses should consider doing next.
