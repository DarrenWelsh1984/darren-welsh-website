Priority service

Post-clearance audit and compliance review support.

A lot of customs risk is not visible at the point goods move. It appears later — during internal reviews, customer queries, HMRC checks, post-clearance audits or when historic declarations are revisited.

01

Declaration Reviews

Reviewing customs entries, commercial documents and supporting records to identify errors, patterns and areas of exposure.

02

Audit Preparation

Helping businesses prepare for customs audits by checking processes, documentation, data quality and record keeping.

03

Corrective Action

Turning audit findings into practical actions — improving processes, reducing repeat errors and strengthening compliance controls.
